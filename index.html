About

I'm Mel John Macabenta, a computer science graduate drawn to the practical side of machine learning and AI — building systems, measuring whether they actually work, and shipping the ones that do.

My undergraduate thesis put nine retrieval-augmented generation configurations through a structured evaluation to find the most reliable setup for an institutional chatbot. The rest of my work spans chatbots, inventory systems, and the occasional game. Across all of it, I care about software that's accurate, usable, and honest about its limits.
